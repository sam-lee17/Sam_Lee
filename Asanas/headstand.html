Sirsasana (Headstand)

1. Start with both your knees on the ground.
2. Put your elbows on the ground in front of your knees, and interlock your fingers.
3. Create a triangle shape with your forearms. Make sure your wrists are striaght.
4. Place your head between your wrists, gently putting force onto your wrtsts with your head, and vice versa.
5. Lift your knees off the ground and slowly walk toward your elbows until your back is perpendicular to the ground.
6. Use your core and back muscles to lift your feet above the ground.
7. Push the ground with your elbows for stability and straigthen your back and shoulders. During this process, you should always be using your core and back muscles.

Ekapada Rajakapotasana (Pigeon pose)

1. Start with your both knees on the ground and hands next to them.
2. Slide your left knees forward that your thigh and calf will form a 45 angle, and striagthen your right leg backward.
3. Make sure your weight is balanced on both sides, and keep hips square.
4. Bend your right leg and wrap around your toes with your right palm. Slowly lower your foot towards your hip or even toward the ground.

Modification
5. Straigthen your right leg half way so that your lower leg is perpendicualr to the ground.
6.Make a ring with your second and thrid fingers, and wrap your frist toe with your second finger, supporting it with your thrid finger.
7. Rotate your shoulder counter-clock wise that your elbow reaches back of the head.
8. Hold your toes with both hands and slowly move your foot towards your forehead, trying to touch it.
9. Stick your elbows together.

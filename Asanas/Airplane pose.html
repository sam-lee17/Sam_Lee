Airplane Pose

Partner 1 (Bottom)
1. Lie down on your back.
2. Put your feet on Partner 2's hipbone and lift him/her up into the air, striagthening both legs.
3. Hold your Partner 2's hands and straigthen both arms.
4. Make sure your legs are perpendicular to the ground.

Partner 2 (Top)
1. When Partner 1 lifts you up into the air, kick off the ground for an additional momentum at the same time.
2. Hold Partner 1's hands and straigthen both arms.
3. Try to stretch your entire body like a flat surface.

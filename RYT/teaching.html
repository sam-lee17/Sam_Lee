My Thoughts on Yoga Teaching Methodologies

August 14, 2018
Other(Teaching)

QUALITIES

Qualities that yoga teachers should embody:

- authenticity

- be humble

- be compassionate and show concern for the students

- have a consistent practice

- have a teacher/mentor

- lead by examples

- challenge the student

- nonattachment

 

My yoga teacher embodies all of these qualities. She is compassionate but maintains a neutral stance at the same time. She doesn’t push us out of our boundaries if we don’t want the challenge but helps us to go out of our comfort zone if we want to. She guides us through our journey as an example that we could follow. I also think that yoga teachers should be steady, unwavering, and consistent with the students, their practice, and the energy they consist.

 

BOUNDARIES

A yoga class should be a safe environment for people. In its literal meaning, the classroom should not have any safety hazards or things out in the aisle that could trip someone. It should also provide the feeling of respect and freedom with certain boundaries. It is a yoga teacher’s duty to set a safe boundary regarding one’s relationship with the students and individual expression of thoughts and emotions. A yoga teacher should remain impartial at all times.

 

ENVIRONMENT

I would use music only during savasana, which helps students to meditate and ground themselves. It is crucial to arrive early and ground myself before I teach the students because my energy level significantly affects them. In my class, the mats would face each other to walk around the class, easily giving adjustments.
